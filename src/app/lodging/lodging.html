Lodging

Taniti Resort

The four-star Taniti Resort has everything to make your stay on Taniti Island enjoyable and convenient. With spacious rooms, delicious cuisine, and friendly staff, you won't want to go home.

Family-Owned Hotels

You can support local businesses by staying at one of our many family-owned hotels. The rooms are adorned in traditional Tanitian decor, and you can enjoy yummy Tanitian cuisine. Don't worry, if you prefer a familiar menu, our hotels serve many American-style options.

Private Stays

Are you interested in a more private stay? Then book a stay at a private beach house, condo, or home. You can come and go at your leisure and won't have to worry about other guests (it's just you and your space).

Bed and Breakfasts

We also have many bed and breakfasts for those seeking a cozy stay. Enjoy local hosting, customs, and cuisine for an unforgettable experience.
